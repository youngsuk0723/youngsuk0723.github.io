Internship Opportunities

About internship

ML and Forecast science team at AWS AI Labs is looking for motivated research interns for summer 2021 (and autumn). The internship is minimum 12-14 weeks and requires PhD candidacy in relevant areas. The main goal of this internship is to develop scalable and reliable time series analysis methods, reflecting various empirical or theoretical machine learning topics, along with some publication artifacts in venues of top-tier conferences or journals at the end of internship.

About ML and Forecast Science team in AWS AI Labs

Regarding to our team, please refer to our recent work in selected papers, and AWS AI Forecast.

Ideal candidates

have a strong background in one or more of the following machine learning areas, but not limited to,

  • Seq2Seq deep learning models (RNN, Transformer)

  • generative model (GAN, VAE, normalizing flow)

  • reinforcement learning (off-policy), robust optimization

  • explainable AI, causal inference, distrbution shift, fairness

  • autoML (meta learning, transfer learning, domain adaptation)

and preferably have publication records in in top-tier ML/AI conferences, e.g., ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR, AISTATS, KDD

Applications

If you are interested, feel free to send youngsuk@cs.stanford.edu an email attached with your C.V. and short introduction
