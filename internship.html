Internship Opportunities

About internship

Forecast science team at AWS AI Labs, are looking for motivated research interns in coming summer 2021. The internship is minimum 12 weeks and requires PhD candidacy in relevant areas. Regarding to general info of our team, please refer to selected papers, GluonTS repo, AWS AI page. The main goal of this internship is to develop scalable and reliable time-series forecasting tasks, reflecting various topics possibly across machine learning, statistics, and optimization, along with some publication artifacts in venues of top-tier conferences or journals.

Ideal candidates

have a strong background on one or more of the following areas

  • Seq2Seq deep learning models

  • generative model (GAN, VAE, normalizing flow)

  • autoML (meta learning, transfer learning, domain adaptation)

  • reinforcement learning (off-policy)

  • time-series forecasting and anomaly detection

  • other theoretical topics, e.g., fairness, explainability, distribution shift, model misspecification

have strong at least one publication

  • in top-tier AI conferences, e.g. ICML, NeurIPS, AISTATS, ICLR, KDD

  • preferably as a first author
