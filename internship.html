Internship Opportunities

About internship

Forecast science team is looking for motivated research interns coming summer 2021. The internship is minimum 12 weeks and requires PhD candidacy in relevant areas. The main goal of this internship is to develop scalable and reliable time-series forecasting methods, reflecting various empirical or theoretical machine learning topics, along with some publication artifacts in venues of top-tier conferences or journals at the end of internship.

About Amazon Forecast science team

Regarding to our team, please refer to selected papers, GluonTS repo, and Amazon Forecast.

Ideal candidates

have a strong background in one or more of the following machine learning areas, but not limited to,

  • Seq2Seq deep learning models

  • generative model (GAN, VAE, normalizing flow)

  • autoML (meta learning, transfer learning, domain adaptation)

  • time-series forecasting and anomaly detection

  • stochastic process, Neural process, Neural ODE

  • reinforcement learning (off-policy)

  • other theoretical topics, e.g., fairness, explainability, distribution shift, conformal inference, causal inference.

and preferably have at least one publication

  • in top-tier AI conferences, e.g. ICML, NeurIPS, AISTATS, ICLR, KDD

Applications

If you are interested, feel free to send pyoungsu@amazon.com an email attached with your C.V. and short introduction.
