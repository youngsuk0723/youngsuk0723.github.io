Internship Opportunities

About Internship

ML and Forecast science team at AWS AI Labs is looking for motivated research interns for summer 2022. The internship is minimum 12-14 weeks and requires PhD candidacy in relevant areas. The main goal of this internship is to develop scalable and reliable time series analysis methods, reflecting various empirical or theoretical machine learning topics, along with some publication artifacts in venues of top-tier conferences at the end of internship.

About ML and Forecast Science team in AWS AI Labs

Regarding to our team, please refer to our recent work in selected papers.

Ideal Quality of Candidates

Strong background in one or more of the following machine learning areas, but not limited to,

  • explainable AI, causal inference,

  • large scale representation learning for foundation model,

  • robustness, distribution shift, OOD,

  • reinforcement learning for decision making

  • GNN for temporal data

preferably with publication records in in top-tier ML/AI conferences, e.g., ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR, AISTATS, KDD.

Applications

If you are interested, feel free to send youngsuk@cs.stanford.edu an email attached with your C.V. and short introduction.
